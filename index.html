About

From business owner to dealmaker

In 2012, my wife Karson and I bought a yoga studio in San Diego, grew it into a thriving community business, and sold it in 2019 for five times what we paid. That experience — seeing a business from every side as an owner, operator, buyer, and seller — became the foundation for everything I do now.

I created Boutique Fitness Broker in 2020. It's the only M&A advisory focused entirely on boutique fitness, health, wellness, and service businesses — yoga, Pilates, Lagree, spa, recovery, and beyond. These are passion-led, owner-operator businesses where community and retention are everything.

The patterns I've seen across 70+ deals apply far beyond fitness. I help business owners envision their next chapter — and I deliver an engaging, actionable session that event planners can count on to leave a lasting impact on the room.

The Book

The Number Nobody Talks About

For business owners who are starting to think about what's next — and want to be ready when the time comes.
