About

From studio owner to dealmaker

In 2012, my wife Karson and I bought our favorite little yoga studio in San Diego, grew it into a thriving community business, and sold it in 2019 for five times what we paid. Those experiences — seeing the business and industry from every side — became the foundation for everything I do now.

I created Boutique Fitness Broker in 2020. It's the only M&A firm focused entirely on boutique fitness, health and wellness — such as yoga, Pilates and Lagree as well as spa, wellness and recovery. These passion-led owner-operator businesses live and die on community and retention.

I help owners and future owners envision that incredible life transition — and I deliver an engaging, actionable session that event planners can trust will inspire, energize, and leave a lasting impact on everyone in the room.

The Book

The Number Nobody Talks About

For boutique fitness studio owners who are starting to think about what's next — and want to make sure they're ready when the time comes.

You've built something real. You know what it took. And at some point — maybe now, maybe in two years — you're going to want to turn that into something that actually changes your financial life.

The owners who get the best outcomes don't wing it when they decide to sell. They've been making the right decisions for a year or two before anyone even knew they were thinking about it. That's the window this book is about.

I've been on every side of these deals — as a studio owner, a buyer, and now the broker. What I've seen is that the owners who get life-changing exits aren't luckier. They just started preparing earlier. This book shows you exactly how.

The Number Nobody Talks About is your exit number — the one most owners never figure out how to reach. This book is how you get there.
